About

Rikesh Khadka is the Founder and Chairman of RISI Global, a holding group whose operating companies span Nepal and the United States, each incorporated and registered under its own jurisdiction.

He holds a B.Sc. in Agricultural Engineering from Tribhuvan University, Nepal, and is a Nepal Engineering Council registered engineer (Category A, No. 271). He has over seven years of construction project management experience across Nepal and Japan, and currently serves as Coordinator, NRNA Japan — Fukuoka Chapter.
